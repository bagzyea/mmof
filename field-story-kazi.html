How Kazi Health grew from 3 to 11 branches.

Strong demand, thin systems, and a founder doing everything. We put in a growth facility and an operator — then built the multi-site engine underneath the business.

11
branches, up from 3

The arc, in three moves.

When we invested
A $1.2M growth facility in 2022 — three clinics, strong demand, thin systems.
What we built
An embedded operator for 14 months — finance, clinical SOPs, a hiring engine and a multi-site playbook.
Where they are now
Eleven branches across two counties, 2.1× revenue, 240 new jobs — and profitable.
