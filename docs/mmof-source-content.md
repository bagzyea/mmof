## Who MMOF actually is
- **MMOF = The Missing Middle Opportunity Facility.**
- Provides **Business Development Services (BDS) + capital "under the same roof"** to
  sustainable SMEs in low-income countries.
- HQ: **Helsinki, Finland** (Eteläranta 8, 00130 Helsinki). Offices in **Uganda, Kenya, Somalia**.
- Positioning: solve the **"Missing Middle"** — SMEs too big for microloans, too small for
  development banks, lacking the *combination* of BDS + finance.
- Contact: **info@mmof.com**, LinkedIn. Contact form fields: Full name, Email, Message.

## Case studies (3 real — replace the 6 fake portfolio companies)

### AMFRI Farms Ltd — Uganda — Organic agriculture (fruits, vegetables, spices)
- Exports organic food to EU, USA, UAE. 6 owned farms, 300 employees, 560 certified smallholder
  farms (smallholders = 80% of production). Predominantly youth, ~40% women.
- **Challenge:** finance a new processing facility closer to Kampala / Entebbe cargo hub.
- **Solution:** **$1.3M** joint investment with Yield Fund Uganda + BDS (financing, accounting,
  marketing, sales).
- **Results:** new facility, diversified higher-margin products, planned >100% expansion, jobs.

### Egg Production Uganda Ltd (EPL) — Uganda — Poultry / eggs
- Mityana District; est. 2019; founded by Women's Bank (FCA Group). Targets 25,000 layers;
  integrates smallholder maize farmers; 14 youth employed.
- **Challenge:** COVID-19 — economic closure, staff infections, construction delays, price crash.
- **Solution:** a **"service-backed loan"** — BDS (production planning, financial management,
  go-to-market) + capital.
- **Results:** turnaround, moving to premium segment, structured GTM, long-term contracts, focus
  on female smallholder livelihoods.

### Sakami Ranches Ltd — Kenya — Agriculture (coffee, macadamia, avocado)
- Near Kitale; est. 2004; family-owned; grower's export license, direct EU sales; 100+ women in
  peak picking season.
- **Challenge:** coffee-market volatility, inconsistent yields; needed to diversify + improve water mgmt.
- **Solution:** capital + BDS — expanded 100+ acres, 2 water reservoirs, solar pumps, modern
  equipment, financial/operational analysis, riparian forest restoration, neighbour-farm partnerships.
- **Results:** +100 acres production, solar water savings, efficiency gains, premium-market products.
